About Pitchus.

Connecting visionary startups with forward-thinking investors through AI-powered matchmaking

Our Mission

At Pitchus, we believe that every great idea deserves a chance to succeed. Our mission is to democratize access to funding and create a thriving ecosystem where innovative startups can connect with the right investors.

Founded in 2023 by a team of entrepreneurs and investors who experienced the fundraising challenges firsthand, we set out to build a platform that simplifies the investment process while leveraging AI to enhance pitch quality.
